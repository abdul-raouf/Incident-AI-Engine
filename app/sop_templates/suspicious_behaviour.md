# SOP: Suspicious Behaviour

## Immediate Actions
- Do NOT approach or confront the individual
- Alert security via radio — describe: {{parties_involved}}
- Monitor via CCTV at: {{location}}

## Containment
- Keep subject in sight without alarming them
- Note time of observation: {{incident_time}}

## Reporting
- Log full description: appearance, behaviour, direction of travel
- Submit report to security supervisor within 30 minutes

## Escalation
- Contact police if threat level increases
- Preserve all CCTV footage: {{additional_context}}
