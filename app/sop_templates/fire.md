# SOP: Fire Incident

## Immediate Actions
- Activate the nearest fire alarm
- Evacuate all personnel from: {{location}}
- Call Civil Defense: 997

## Containment
- Use fire extinguisher ONLY if fire is small and contained
- Do not use elevators during evacuation
- Incident detected at: {{incident_time}}

## Reporting
- Account for all staff at muster point
- Do not re-enter until cleared by Civil Defense

## Escalation
- Notify RTA Emergency Operations Center immediately
- Preserve any evidence of fire origin: {{additional_context}}
