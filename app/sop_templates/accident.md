# SOP: Accident / Collision

## Immediate Actions
- Ensure scene safety — do not move injured persons
- Call ambulance if injuries reported: {{injuries_mentioned}}
- Secure the area at: {{location}}

## Documentation
- Photograph vehicle positions and damage if safe
- Note time of incident: {{incident_time}}
- Collect details of parties involved: {{parties_involved}}

## Reporting
- Complete RTA Accident Report Form within 2 hours
- Notify traffic control if road is blocked

## Escalation
- Escalate to RTA Operations if fatality or major injury
